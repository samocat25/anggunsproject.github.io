Welcome to My Pocket

My Pocket is a simple financial management app designed to help you track your daily income and expenses with ease. Follow the guide below to get started and take control of your finances.

How To Use My Pocket?

  1. Make an account or Login through Login.
  2. Go to the Transactions page to record a new transaction.
  3. Fill out the transaction form completely, including the transaction name, amount, category, and date.
  4. Click the Save button to save the transaction.
  5. View your recorded transactions on the Transaction List page.
  6. Learn more about the application on the Profile page.
