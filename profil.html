About My Pocket

My Pocket is a simple and user-friendly personal finance tracker designed to help you manage your daily finances with ease. Whether you're recording your income, tracking expenses, or monitoring your savings, My Pocket keeps all your financial records organized in one convenient place.

The application allows users to add transactions, categorize them as Income, Expense, or Savings, and view their transaction history anytime. With its clean interface and straightforward features, My Pocket makes financial management accessible for everyone, including students and beginners.

Our goal is to encourage better financial habits by providing an easy way to monitor cash flow and make informed spending decisions. By keeping track of your finances regularly, you can stay organized, save more effectively, and achieve your financial goals.

My Pocket – Track Smart. Spend Wisely. Save Better.
